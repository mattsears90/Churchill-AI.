Permits & Cemetery Rules

Cemetery approval made easier.

Most cemeteries and churchyards require permission before installation or alteration.

What needs checking?

Grave ownershipThe deed holder or authorised person normally approves work.
Memorial size and materialRules may affect height, width, stone type, finish, colour and shape.
Inscription wordingSome cemeteries and churchyards restrict wording, symbols and images.
Fixing and foundationInstallation must meet cemetery standards and fixing requirements.
Application paperworkDesign, inscription, grave details and ownership information may be submitted.
Permit feesFees vary by location and are checked before the final quote proceeds.
